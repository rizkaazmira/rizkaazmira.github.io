about

I'm a data scientist currently building experimentation platform at an Indonesian tech company. My work involves statistical frameworks, methodology, and A/B testing pipelines — the kind of infrastructure that makes it possible to know whether anything you shipped actually worked.

I have a weird obsession with symbols and metaphors. Most of my time is spent trying to compress things to shapes I can hold in my head.

I believe that understanding comes from making patterns visible that were previously hidden. The best discoveries don't just work — they make previously illegible things legible.
